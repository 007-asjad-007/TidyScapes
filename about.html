About Our Company

Tidy Scapes, led by owner Benjamin Gaunt, is a premier landscaping company based in Dacula, Georgia, dedicated to transforming outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional environments. Our comprehensive range of services is tailored to meet the unique needs of both residential and commercial clients.

Our Services

  • Landscape Maintenance: Keeping your outdoor environment pristine and healthy requires consistent care. Our landscape maintenance services include mowing, edging, weeding, mulching, and seasonal clean-ups. Whether its removing leaves in the fall or preparing your garden beds in the spring, we ensure your landscape always looks its best.
  • Shrub Care: Shrubs are essential elements of any well-designed landscape, and their health matters. We provide expert care for all types of shrubs, ensuring they remain vibrant and healthy year-round. This includes monitoring for pests, diseases, and nutrient deficiencies.
  • Shrub Pruning or Trimming: Proper pruning not only enhances the appearance of shrubs but also promotes their health and growth. Our team uses precise trimming techniques to shape and maintain your shrubs, ensuring they complement the overall design of your landscape.
  • Shrub Transplanting: Relocating shrubs can be a delicate process, and we handle it with expertise. Whether you are redesigning your landscape or giving your shrubs a better location to thrive, we ensure a smooth transplant process that prioritizes the health of your plants.
  • Tree Planting: Trees bring life, shade, and beauty to any property. Our tree planting services help you choose the right species for your soil, climate, and design goals, and we ensure proper planting techniques to encourage long-term growth and health.
  • Lawn Care: A lush, green lawn is often the centerpiece of a landscape. Our lawn care services include fertilization, aeration, seeding, and weed control. We work to ensure your lawn stays healthy, vibrant, and free of pests or diseases, providing a perfect space for relaxation and play.
  • Shrub Planting: Strategic shrub planting can add depth, color, and texture to your outdoor space. Our experts guide you in selecting the right shrubs for your property and ensure they are planted with care to flourish in their new environment.
  • Shrub Removal: If your shrubs are overgrown, diseased, or no longer complement your landscape, we provide safe and efficient shrub removal services. We handle the process with care, ensuring minimal disruption to the surrounding areas.
  • Tree Care Services: Healthy trees are vital for a beautiful and sustainable landscape. We provide comprehensive tree care services, including pruning, disease management, and overall maintenance, to ensure your trees remain strong and vibrant for years to come.
